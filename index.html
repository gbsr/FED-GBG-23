Welcome to FEX 2023
A conference for Frontend Developers, by Frontend Developers.

Get ready to dig deep into the beautiful world of Frontend Development

In the small, beautiful, but very windy town of Gothenburg, Sweden, we are all about Accessibility this year. For example, since some people have color-blindness, we've decided for this years page to include a ridiculous amount of colors.

This way we can (probably) deliver colors that everyone can appreciate!

We've also included a small white text-shadow on the text to make it more legible. If you squint hard enough, I'm sure you can see it. Don't worry if you don't know what that means. This is we are here:

To help you level up your skills in Frontend Development

Talks for this year is focused on accessibility, responsive design, and the most common layouts.

Of course, we don't care about the most common layouts, because why should we? We decided to go full on late 90's, early 2000 scrap book style instead, because.. why not?

(Breaking the rules is ok)

Nevertheless, the talks we're hosting is all about Better Web™ and wants to make sure that we (or you, as it is) follow both the rules and the most common design principles, so get your ticket now and
conform!

FEX

FEX is a conference for Frontend Developers, meticulously crafted by Frontend Developers

As the creators of FEX, we are very proud of our lineage. It all started as two developers started having Hackathons every other week. We sat in coffee-shops. We coded at bars. We spent time in each other's apartments.

As time went by, more developers wanted to join and suddenly we realized that neither the apartments, the bars, nor the coffe-shops were big enough. Suddenly we had a bi-weekly meet-up and it was growing.

We needed more.

More space.

More challenges.

More people.

As time went by we continued to grow, and so did our requirements. No longer was a bi-weekly hackathon a thing, where people gathered and created something random during 3 days time.

People started to ask if they could hold seminars and talks about their creations. Participants started to join just to join for the talks, and not develop anything, as per the previous Hackathon 'rules'.

This is when we knew we were on to something big

FEX was finally born!

Our first ever real conference was held at Eriksbergs Conference Hall in 2021, and hosted 25 talks and more than 500 visitors!
