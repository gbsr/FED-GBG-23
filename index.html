Welcome to FEX 2023
A conference for Frontend Developers, by Frontend Developers.

This year is all about Better Web™

For example, since some people have color-blindness, we've decided to include a ridiculous amount of colors in this landing page.

You've probably noticed by now. That's all good and all. We've also included a small white text-shadow on the text to make it more legible.

If you squint hard enough, I'm sure you can see it.

Talks for this year is focused on accessibility, responsive design, and the most common layouts.

Of course, we don't care about the most common layouts, because why should we?

We decided to go full on late 90's, early 2000 scrap book style instead, because.. why not?

(Breaking the rules is ok)

Nevertheless, the talks we're hosting wants to make sure that we follow both the rules and the most common design principles, so get your ticket now and conform!
